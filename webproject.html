WHAT IS WELLNESS?

Wellness is the ability to be responsible for our own choices, behaviors and lifestyles. Is is also significantly influenced by the physical, social and cultural environments in which we live.

Now more than ever it is imperative that we take control of our state of mind and holistically implement routines that foster a healthy lifestyle.

10 SELF CARE TIPS

  • Go for a run.
  • Spend quality time with your family.
  • Spend more time outside.
  • Eat right.
  • Learn a new skill.
  • Quit negative habits.
  • Practice gratitude.
  • Remove toxic friends.
  • Love yourself.
  • Spend some alone time.

WELLNESS IS MULTI-DIMENSIONAL

Wellness is not only limited to eating well and exercising.

It helps to be spiritually connected to a higher Being or establish a connection to the Divine.
Prayer and mindfulness allows you develop a sense of peace and serenity that calms the soul.



Spending time in nature improves your well-being. It is no surprise why people are fond of chasing sunrises and sunsets. It is exhilirating.

FINAL THOUGHTS ON WELLNESS

By incorporating a few of these practises in your life, together with volunteering your time and specific strengths to your community you are able to lead a more fulfilling life with purpose and gratitude
